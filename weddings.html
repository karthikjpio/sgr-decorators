Exquisite Wedding Decor.

From grand mandaps to enchanting stage setups, we craft unforgettable wedding atmospheres — blending tradition with modern design, tailored to your vision.

Bespoke mandap designs, breathtaking florals, ambient lighting, and seamless coordination — every wedding we style blends South Indian tradition with contemporary elegance, tailored to your vision from first consultation to final farewell.

Mandap & Stage Floral Design Lighting Draping & Tenting Full Event Styling

Mandap & Stage

Bespoke Mandaps, Rooted in Tradition

Bespoke mandap designs that blend tradition with modern aesthetics. We create sacred spaces that reflect your personal style and cultural heritage — from grand carved structures to custom themed mandaps built for destination celebrations.

Floral Design

Breathtaking Florals, Tailored to You

Exquisite floral arrangements for mandaps, stages, aisles and centerpieces. We use fresh, vibrant flowers — marigolds, roses and orchids — to create stunning visual impacts tailored to your theme and preferences.

Lighting

Magical Ambiances, Lit to Perfection

From subtle mood lighting to dazzling displays — LEDs, chandeliers, string lights and more. Our creative lighting designs transform any venue into a magical setting, day or night.

Draping & Tenting

Elegant Spaces, Indoors or Out

Elegant shamiyanas, tents and fabric draping for indoor and outdoor setups — perfect for creating comfortable, stylish event spaces in any season or venue.

Full Event Styling

Every Detail, Planned and Coordinated

Venue layout planning, furniture and linen selection, props and accessories, and on-site coordination — meticulously planning and executing every aspect of the decoration, start to finish.
