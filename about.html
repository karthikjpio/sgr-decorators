A Legacy Built by Family

SGR Decorators was founded in 1999 by Javanappa, born from a passion for transforming spaces and celebrating life’s special moments. His vision, combined with an unwavering commitment to quality and creativity, established SGR Decorators as a trusted name in event decoration across Bengaluru.

Today, that legacy continues under his son, Deepak Jp, who leads the company as Managing Director — carrying forward his father’s values while bringing fresh creative energy to every celebration. Over 27+ years, we’ve had the privilege of decorating thousands of events across the city and beyond, one celebration at a time.
